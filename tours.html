Fearless Tour: The beginning of the sparkle. Her first headlining tour, featuring theatrical performances and surprise guest appearances. It was a mix of country and pop, with hits like Love Story and You Belong With Me.
Speak Now Tour: Pure fairytale magic. A visually stunning tour with elaborate set designs, including a floating balcony and a fairytale-inspired theme. It showcased her storytelling ability with songs like Enchanted and Mean.
Red Tour: A fiery emotional rollercoaster. Marked her transition into pop, featuring high-energy performances and surprise duets with artists like Ed Sheeran. The tour was known for its emotional depth, highlighted by All Too Well.
1989 Tour: The pop queen's global takeover. A full-fledged pop spectacle with massive production, LED wristbands, and celebrity cameos. It solidified her status as a global pop icon.
Reputation Tour: She held her head high. Her first all-stadium tour, featuring a darker, edgier aesthetic with giant snakes and pyrotechnics. It broke records for the highest-grossing North American tour.
City of Lover: Paris meets poetry.
Eras Tour: The grandest celebration ever. A career-spanning tour celebrating all of her musical eras, with a setlist covering every album. It became one of the highest-grossing tours in history.
