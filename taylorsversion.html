After Scooter sold Taylor's masters, she couldn’t get them back, so she re-recorded them with new vault songs — some even got music videos.
Fearless TV was announced in February 2021. "Love Story (Taylor's Version)" was the first single, and the album released on April 9, 2021, including 6 vault tracks.
Red TV was announced on June 18, 2021. It was released on November 19, 2021, including all 30 songs originally intended, plus 9 vault tracks.
1989 TV was announced during the Eras Tour in LA on August 9, 2023. It released on October 27 with 5 new vault tracks.
Speak Now TV was announced in Nashville on May 5, 2023. It released on July 7, 2023 — also National Koi Fish Day!
